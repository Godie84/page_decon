About DECON LLC

Multidisciplinary Engineering Expertise

DECON LLC is a Houston-based, multidisciplinary engineering firm specializing in civil, structural, and architectural design solutions for both public and private sector clients across Texas and throughout the United States.

Since our founding in 2010, DECON has established a strong reputation for delivering innovative, resilient, and cost-effective engineering solutions that enhance the built environment and uphold the highest standards of safety, functionality, and sustainability.

Licensed in 11 States

P.E. credentials in Texas, Georgia, Virginia, Florida, Oklahoma, Louisiana, Tennessee, Maryland, North Carolina, DC, South Carolina, Alabama, and Utah

DBE/MBE Certified

TxDOT Pre-certified in multiple work categories including bridge design, roadway design, and construction engineering

Trusted Partner

Proven track record with DOTs, toll authorities, and private developers across the United States
