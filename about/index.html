15 Years of Engineering Excellence

DECON LLC is a Houston-based, multidisciplinary engineering firm specializing in civil, structural, and architectural design solutions for clients across Texas and the United States.

Who We Are

Since our founding in 2010, DECON has established a strong reputation for delivering innovative, resilient, and cost-effective engineering solutions that enhance the built environment and uphold the highest standards of safety, functionality, and sustainability.

Our team brings together extensive experience in Design-Build, Public-Private Partnerships (P3), and traditional design projects, serving Departments of Transportation, toll authorities, municipalities, and private developers.
